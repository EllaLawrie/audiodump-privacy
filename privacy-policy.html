AudioDump Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AudioDump collects:

  • Account information (name, email) when you register
  • Audio recordings you choose to transcribe
  • Transcriptions and text outputs
  • Purchase history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide audio transcription services
  • Store your transcription history
  • Process purchases via Apple In-App Purchase

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely. Audio recordings are processed and immediately deleted after transcription. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.
